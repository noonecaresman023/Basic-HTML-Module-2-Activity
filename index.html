A Quick Guide on the Miata!

Reasons to buy a Miata!

  • Small car; will fit in most parking spaces
  • It looks cool!
  • It's a Miata and fun to drive!

Steps to maintain your Miata!

  1. Cleaning!
    • Hand wash it! (Preferably out of the sun)
    • Dry it w/ towel
  2. Drive Carefully
  3. Take Routine Maintenance Seriously!
  4. Cover with Paint Protection Film!

Which Miata should you buy?

  • NA(1989-1997)
    • It is iconic because of it's pop-up headlights!
    • Very old!
  • NB(1998-2005)
    • Sadly, the disappearance of the pop-up headlights!
    • Not as popular and iconic as the NA
  • NC(2006-2015)
    • Better than the NA and NB
    • Introduction of the PRHT model which meant better security
    • Roomy interior and it looks happy and smiling on later years
  • ND(2016-2023~)
    • Fancier and modern features
    • A Targa top :( or :)
    • Beautiful Soul Red Color!
